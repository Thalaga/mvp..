Our Services

Saver

This option provides basic storage. It is ideal if you just need a safe place to store your luggage for a short period. The Saver plan is affordable and gives you the flexibility to add services like pickup, delivery, and optional insurance for additional peace of mind.

R80/hour or R1000/day

What's Included: Secure storage, flexible pickup and delivery (optional), optional insurance.

Ideal For: Travelers who need temporary storage for a short period.

Flexi

Flexi provides the same secure storage but with added benefits. It includes 50% insurance coverage to protect your belongings, along with pickup and delivery. In case of delays, we compensate for late deliveries. The perfect option for travelers with high-value items who want extra protection.

R120/hour or R1400/day

What's Included: Secure storage, 50% insurance coverage, pickup and delivery, late delivery compensation.

Ideal For: Travelers with valuable items or those looking for more comprehensive coverage and flexibility.
